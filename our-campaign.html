Our Vision

To build an India that is self-sufficient, healthy and prosperous by creating model villages that provides its communities livelihood opportunities, health, education and a quality of life

Our Mission

Focus on building the Villages of Raigad District in Maharashtra, ‘Block –by- Block’ by empowering the local communities to work with the natural resources while preserving the environment

Our Strategy

Jai Jawan, Jai Kissan, Jai Vidhwan. Build National Pride, preserve the Environment and protect the Farmers and local Trades by leveraging natural resources. Bring local people into Government Jobs

Our Belief

“I am because we are.” Inspired by the African Belief in the concept of “UBUNTU”. Only a group of people who support each other on a quest can make the world better.

About Us

Founders’ speak

The recent turn of events brought about by COVID-19 forces us to re-examine Mahatma Gandhi’s sage assertion that

“The future of India lives in her villages”.

The current situation has forced workers to return to rural areas. This reverse migration has impacted our economy and wreaked havoc on millions of lives.

That it will further impact livelihoods, health, women empowerment, the education of the children of migrants and a huge number of vulnerable communities in a variety of ways, is not just forecast but a tragic certainty.

Our Focus areas and Activities

JAVAB will focus on RAIGAD District in Maharashtra which is in close proximity to both Mumbai and Pune and is one of the 35 districts of Maharashtra State. It is divided into 15 tehsils namely Uran, Panvel, Karjat, Khalapur, Pen, Alibag, Murud, Roha, Tala, Sudhagad, Mangaon, Shrivardhan, Mhasla, Mahad and Poladpur.

Raigad district is divided into four subdivisions, with fifteen talukas, and 1,967 villages.

The district includes cities/towns Kharghar, Taloja, Kalamboli, Panvel, Rasayani, Karjat, Khopoli, Matheran, Uran, Pen, Alibag, Murud-Janjira, Roha, Nagothane, Sudhagad-Pali, Mangaon, Mhasla, Shrivardhan, Mahad, Birwadi, Poladpur. The largest city in terms of Population, Industrialization as well as Area is Panvel.
