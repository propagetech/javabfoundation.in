OUR OBJECTIVE

The objective is to make a difference by providing Relief to the Poor and Underprivileged and creating an impact on their lives by focussing on the Economic, Social, Educational & Infrastructural needs of the Villages.

Economic

To help create more self -employment opportunities particularly for unemployed youth and women.

To strengthen primary occupations like agriculture and animal husbandry through increased output & value addition.

To create value added products through recycling and upcycling

To strengthen secondary occupations such as cottage industry, handicrafts and service sector through quality enhancement and wider market acceptability.

Educational

Train youth for skill sets that are applicable to the local Industries eg factories near Khopoli and Khalapur Toll point

Train Women for self-sustainability and the Hospitality sector – poultry & prawn farming, growing Salads and ready Masala packets.

Provide Educational counselling, guidance and training for IPS/ Bank jobs/ Army/ Sports Quota for Jobs

Social

To develop proper social, cultural, scientific and spiritual attitude amidst rural community.

To instil in villagers an urge and keenness to work for their own development and take ownership for the development of their Villages.

To develop an attitude towards living a healthy life and taking concrete steps in that direction.

Use sports and playgrounds to create team spirit, social connect, health and build leadership skills.

Infrastructure

To create basic infrastructure facilities for the community such as:
  • Provision for drinking water.
  • Improve the water table.
  • Building internal roads
  • Basic sanitation
  • Formal education
  • Playgrounds and Community centers
  • Solar Electrification
  • Training cum production centers
